Best flowers for an apology

For hundreds of years, the act of giving flowers has become an unspoken language for people around the world. Whether you want to say ‘I love you’, ‘congratulations’, or a simple ‘thank you’, flowers convey a special message to the recipient that cannot be completed by mere words and actions. But there is one thing that you can’t really express easily, may it be through words or flowers and that is an apology. Of all the things you want to say to a person you love, saying ‘I am sorry’ is never easy because you know you messed up and it would take a lot of effort to fix things up. As what singer Elton John soulfully crooned in his song, sorry, indeed, seems to be the hardest word.
